<body>

Body of the Article

Definition

The main textual portion of the article that conveys the content

Related Elements

A journal article <article> may be divided into three parts:

  1. the <front> (the article metadata or header information, for the article);
  2. the <body> (textual and graphical content of the article); and
  3. any <back> (ancillary information such as a glossary, reference list, or appendix).

Model Information

Content Model

<!ELEMENT  body         %body-model;                                 >

Description

The following, in order:

  • Any combination of:
    • Boxed Text <boxed-text>
    • Chemical Structure Wrapper <chem-struct-wrapper>
    • Figure <fig>
    • Graphic <graphic>
    • Media Object <media>
    • Preformatted Text <preformat>
    • Supplementary Material <supplementary-material>
    • Table Wrapper <table-wrap>
    • Formula, Display <disp-formula>
    • Paragraph <p>
    • Definition List <def-list>
    • List <list>
    • Quote, Displayed <disp-quote>
    • Speech <speech>
    • Statement, Formal <statement>
    • Verse Form for Poetry <verse-group>
  • Section <sec>, zero or more

This element may be contained in:

<article> Article
