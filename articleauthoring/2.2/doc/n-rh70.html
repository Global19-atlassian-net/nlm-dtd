%contrib-info.class;

Contributor Information Class Elements

Definition

Elements that may be used to provide additional information about a Contributor, including external links such as <ext-link> and <uri>, as well as internal links such as <xref>. (For more such elements, see the Parameter Entity %contrib-model;.)

Declaration

<!ENTITY % contrib-info.class
                        "address | aff | author-comment | bio |
                         email | ext-link | on-behalf-of |
                         role | uri | xref"                          >
