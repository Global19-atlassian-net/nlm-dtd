%para-level;

Paragraph-Level Elements

Definition

Elements that may be used at the same structural level as a Paragraph, for example, inside a section

Remarks

Design Note: There is a major overlap between this Parameter Entity and that for the elements that may occur inside a paragraph (%p-elements;). Inline elements appear only inside a paragraph, while block elements, such as quotes and lists, may appear either within a paragraph or at the same level as a paragraph.

Declaration

<!ENTITY % para-level   "%block-display.class; | %block-math.class; |
                         %just-para.class; | %list.class; |
                         %rest-of-para.class;"                       >
