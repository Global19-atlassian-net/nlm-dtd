<issn>

ISSN

Definition

Within bibliographic references (<nlm-citation> and <citation>), International Standard Series Number, an eight-character code that provides title identification for serial publications. Such numbers may be assigned to one or more versions of a journal by the ISDS (International Serials Data System) in Paris or by one of the national ISDS Centers.
