<author-comment>

Author Comment

Definition

An article metadata element that contains extra textual material associated with a contributor (an author or editor)

Remarks

Conversion Note: Although a typical author comment might explain, for example, some facts about the circumstances under which a contributor wrote the article material, during conversion the <author-comment> element can also act as an escape mechanism to take the content of any contributor-related elements not anticipated by the DTD Suite.

Model Information

Content Model

<!ELEMENT  author-comment    
                        %author-comment-model;                       >

Description

The following, in order:

  • Title <title>, zero or one
  • Paragraph <p>, one or more

This element may be contained in:

<contrib> Contributor; <contrib-group> Contributor Group
