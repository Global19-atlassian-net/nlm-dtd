<sig-block>

Signature Block

An area of text and graphic material placed at the end of the body of an article to hold the graphical signature or description of the person(s) responsible for or attesting to the content

Remarks

This element is not a substitute for contributor information in the article metadata.

Attributes

content-type Type of Content
id Identifier
rid Reference to an Identifier
specific-use Specific Use

Content Model

<!ELEMENT  sig-block    (#PCDATA %sig-block-elements;)*              >

Expanded Content Model

(#PCDATA | break | bold | italic | monospace | overline | roman | sans-serif | sc | strike | underline | alternatives | graphic | media | inline-graphic | private-char | named-content | styled-content | sig | sub | sup)*

Description

Any combination of:

  • Text, numbers, or special characters
  • <break> Line Break
  • All the emphasis elements:
    • <bold> Bold
    • <italic> Italic
    • <monospace> Monospace Text (Typewriter Text)
    • <overline> Overline
    • <roman> Roman
    • <sans-serif> Sans Serif
    • <sc> Small Caps
    • <strike> Strike Through
    • <underline> Underline
  • <alternatives> Alternatives For Processing
  • <graphic> Graphic
  • <media> Media Object
  • <inline-graphic> Graphic, Inline
  • <private-char> Private Character (Custom or Unicode™)
  • <named-content> Named Special (Subject) Content
  • <styled-content> Styled Special (Subject) Content
  • <sig> Signature
  • <sub> Subscript
  • <sup> Superscript

This element may be contained in:

<body>

With a graphic representation of a signature: 

...<body>
<sec>
