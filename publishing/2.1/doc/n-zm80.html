<fn-group>

Footnote Group

Definition

Wrapper element for footnotes that have been grouped at the end of an article

Remarks

Footnotes that are scattered throughout the text, at the places they occur, do not use this wrapper element.

Model Information

Content Model

<!ELEMENT  fn-group     %fn-group-model;                             >

Description

The following, in order:

  • Title <title>, zero or one
  • Footnote <fn>, one or more

This element may be contained in:

<app> Appendix; <back> Back Matter; <boxed-text> Boxed Text; <notes> Notes; <sec> Section; <title-group> Title Group
