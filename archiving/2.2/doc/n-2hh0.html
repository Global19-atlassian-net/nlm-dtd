mime-subtype

Mime Subtype

Definition

The mime subtype for a graphic, multimedia object, or piece of supplementary material

Remarks

The source for this attribute’s values is the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA 2003). All permissible non-specific vendor values for mimetype “image” at the time if this DTD’s construction were included.
