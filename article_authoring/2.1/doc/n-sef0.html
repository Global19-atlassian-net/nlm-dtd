%p-elements;

Paragraph Elements

Definition

Elements that may be used within a Paragraph in a mixed content model with data characters (#PCDATA)

Remarks

Design Note: There is a major overlap between this Parameter Entity and that for the elements that are at the same level as a paragraph (%para-level;). Inline elements appear only inside a paragraph, while block elements, such as quotes and lists, may appear either within a paragraph or at the same level as a paragraph.

Declaration

<!ENTITY % p-elements   "| %address-link.class; |
                         %article-link.class; |
                         %block-display.class; |%block-math.class; |
                         %citation.class; | %emphasis.class; |
                         %inline-display.class; |
                         %inline-math.class; | %list.class; |
                         %math.class; | %phrase.class; |
                         %rest-of-para.class; | %simple-link.class; |
                         %subsup.class;"                             >
