<label>

Label (Of a Statement)

Definition

The number and any prefix word associated with the title of a <statement>, such as “Postulate 3.” or “Theorem 2.”

Model Information

Content Model

<!ELEMENT  label        (#PCDATA %label-elements;)*                  >

Description

Any combination of:

  • Text, numbers, or special characters
  • All the emphasis elements:
    • Bold <bold>
    • Italic <italic>
    • Monospace Text (Typewriter Text) <monospace>
    • Overline <overline>
    • Small Caps <sc>
    • Strike Through <strike>
    • Underline <underline>
  • Graphic, Inline <inline-graphic>
  • Formula, Inline <inline-formula>
  • Subscript <sub>
  • Superscript <sup>

This element may be contained in:

<statement> Statement, Formal
