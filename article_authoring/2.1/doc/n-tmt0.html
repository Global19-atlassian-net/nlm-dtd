Article Types

Definition

Lists the explicit values for the article-type attribute, which can be used to attach information classes (such as “book-review”) to articles for grouping or searching

Declaration

<!ENTITY % article-types
                        "abstract | addendum | announcement |
                         article-commentary | book-review |
                         books-received | brief-report | calendar |
                         case-report | correction | discussion |
                         editorial | in-brief | introduction |
                         letter | meeting-report | news | obituary |
                         oration | other | product-review | reply |
                         research-article | retraction |
                         review-article"                             >
