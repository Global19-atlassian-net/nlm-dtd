<collab>

Collaborative (Group) Author

A group of authors, editors, or other contributors credited under a single name, either as a collaboration in the strict sense of the word, or when an organization such as a laboratory, educational institution, corporation, or department acts as the contributor, for example authored the work

Remarks

Not to be confused with the element <contrib-group>, which is a container element that holds information such as the names and affiliations of a group of individual contributors.

This element may be used within bibliographic references <element-citation> and <mixed-citation>.

Attributes

collab-type Type of Collaboration
id Identifier
xlink:actuate Actuating the Link
xlink:href Href (Linking Mechanism)
xlink:role Role of the Link
xlink:show Showing the Link
xlink:title Title of the Link
xlink:type Type of Link
xmlns:xlink XLink Namespace Declaration

Content Model

<!ELEMENT  collab       (#PCDATA %collab-elements;)*                 >

Expanded Content Model

(#PCDATA | bold | italic | monospace | overline | roman | sc | strike | underline | sub | sup | addr-line | country | fax | institution | phone | address | aff | author-comment | bio | email | ext-link | on-behalf-of | role | uri | xref)*

Description

Any combination of:

  • Text, numbers, or special characters
  • All the emphasis elements:
    • <bold> Bold
    • <italic> Italic
    • <monospace> Monospace Text (Typewriter Text)
    • <overline> Overline
    • <roman> Roman
    • <sc> Small Caps
    • <strike> Strike Through
    • <underline> Underline
  • <sub> Subscript
  • <sup> Superscript
  • All the address elements:
    • <addr-line> Address Line
    • <country> Country: in an Address
    • <fax> Fax Number: in an Address
    • <institution> Institution Name: in an Address
    • <phone> Phone Number: in an Address
  • <address> Address/Contact Information
  • <aff> Affiliation
  • <author-comment> Author Comment
  • <bio> Biography
  • All the address linking elements:
    • <email> Email Address
    • <ext-link> External Link
    • <uri> Uniform Resource Indicator (URI)
  • <on-behalf-of> On Behalf of
  • <role> Role or Function Title of Contributor
  • <xref> X(cross) Reference

This element may be contained in:

<contrib>, <element-citation>, <mixed-citation>, <nlm-citation>, <person-group>, <product>, <related-article>, <related-object>

Example 1

...
<contrib-group>
<contrib contrib-type="author">

In an element-style bibliographic citation (punctuation and spacing removed):

...

(US)</collab>

(US)</publisher-name>

</element-citation>
</ref>
...
